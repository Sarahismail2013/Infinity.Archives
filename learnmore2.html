The Story

Tanjiro's Quest & The Tragedy

During Japan's Taisho Era, Tanjiro Kamado lives a humble, peaceful life high in the snowy mountains as a charcoal seller supporting his family. His world shatters in a single afternoon when he returns home to discover his entire family brutally slaughtered by Muzan Kibutsuji, the progenitor of all demons.

His younger sister, Nezuko Kamado, is the sole survivor—but her blood has been infected by Muzan, turning her into a flesh-craving demon. Refusing to abandon her, Tanjiro discovers that Nezuko still retains human empathy, fighting off her demon instincts to protect her brother.

Guided by the Water Hashira Giyu Tomioka, Tanjiro undertakes two years of grueling training under Sakonji Urokodaki to master Water Breathing. He joins the secret Demon Slayer Corps with two ultimate goals: finding a cure to make Nezuko human again, and eradicating Muzan once and for all.

Essential Lore & Worldbuilding

☀️ Sun Breathing (Hinokami Kagura)

Created over 400 years ago by the legendary Yoriichi Tsugikuni, Sun Breathing is the progenitor of all breathing styles in existence. When Yoriichi tried to teach it to other swordsmen, none could master its overwhelming intensity. Thus, he adapted customized variations for them—giving birth to Water, Flame, Wind, Thunder, and Stone Breathing. The original Sun Breathing forms were secretly passed down through generations of the Kamado family as a ceremonial dance known as the Hinokami Kagura.

⚔️ Nichirin Swords (Sun Steel)

Forged high on Mount Yoko by master swordsmiths using Scarlet Crimson Iron Sand and Scarlet Ore, these unique blades constantly absorb solar energy—the only natural force besides sunlight capable of decapitating a demon permanently. When drawn by a skilled warrior, the steel changes color according to the user's innate talent and breathing alignment (e.g., Flame turns red, Water turns blue, and Tanjiro's rare jet-black blade signifies immense unpredictable potential).

🔥 The Demon Slayer Mark

A mysterious, tattoo-like birthmark that manifests on exceptionally talented swordsmen who survive extreme physical conditions (a heart rate exceeding 200 BPM and a body temperature above 39°C/102°F). Once awakened, the Mark drastically enhances physical strength, speed, and perception (granting access to the "Transparent World"), but comes at a grim price: most marked slayers die upon reaching the age of 25.
